About RadiUS

RadiUS is a web-based application that aims to help users find various activities at home or within their vicinity/community. Due to covid people are limited to the distance they can travel until further restrictions are lifted. Using a RadiUs profile account, we aim to help filter activities based on the user's likes and interest.

Using the geological range filters, we can help the user find activities that are closer to the users location.

Users can also create activities and events through the use of a simple form.

Users are able to track their favourite activities.

These events could include but are not limited to: - Community-based events/activities e.g. social gatherings, farmers markets, community sporting events. - Business-based events/activities e.g. dining and cuisine events, ticketed events. - Suggested activities - Suggested movie nights, cooking and hobbies.
